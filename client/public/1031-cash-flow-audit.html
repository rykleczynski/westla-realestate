The math that worked ten years ago doesn't work anymore.

If you're a West LA property owner sitting on meaningful equity, three forces are quietly reshaping the decision to hold or sell. Most agents will tell you one side. I'll show you all three — in 30 minutes.

Your cash flow isn't what it used to be.
