Tenant Screening — The Gold Standard

Not all tenants are created equal. I make sure you get the right one.

A bad tenant costs far more than any leasing fee — in lost rent, legal fees, and property damage. My multi-layer screening process is specifically designed to surface the full picture of every applicant, well beyond what any algorithm can capture.

1
Credit & Background Check — Full credit report, eviction history, and criminal background reviewed against strict benchmarks.
2
Income Verification — Confirmed at 3× monthly rent minimum via pay stubs, bank statements, or employer letters.
3
Rental History Review — Prior landlord references called directly. Not just listed — actually contacted.
4
Personal Reference Checks — Character references verified. Who vouches for this person matters.
5
Face-to-Face Interview — Every finalist meets with me in person. Intuition and professionalism are non-negotiable filters.
